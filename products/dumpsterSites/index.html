// how it works
Three steps. Zero guesswork.
01
Search
Type a business type + ZIP, pick a radius. DumpsterSites hits Google Maps and pulls every matching business in the area. Up to 60 results per query. Wide Area mode tiles overlapping search circles across up to 100 miles — returns 100–300+ leads in one run.

// scoring engine
Nine reasons to knock on their door.
Every factor is a signal. Stack enough of them and you've got a pitch that writes itself.
MOB
No Mobile Viewport
Site has no responsive meta viewport tag. Looks broken on every phone. Instant credibility killer.
SSL
Running on HTTP
No HTTPS in 2024 means browsers are flagging it as insecure. Google is penalizing it too.
TBL
Table-Based Layout
Built with HTML tables for layout. This is a Y2K-era technique. The site needs to be torn down and rebuilt.
PHN
No Phone Number
A local business with no clickable phone number is leaving money on the table every single day.
ADR
No Address on Site
Google can't confirm where they are. Local SEO is dead without it.
CPY
Copyright 3+ Years Old
The footer still says © 2019. Nobody has touched this site since Obama's second term.
CST
Not Custom Coded
Built on Wix, Squarespace, or a site builder. Signals the owner didn't invest — and might be ready to.
SEO
Missing Title / H1 / Meta
The basics of on-page SEO are completely absent. They're invisible to Google.
AEO
No Structured Data / JSON-LD
No schema markup means no rich results, no AI citations, no featured snippets. Zero presence in modern search.
AI
Claude AI Design Grade (A–F)
Claude analyzes the site's visual design, layout, and professionalism. An F here is a gift — it's your pitch in one letter.
